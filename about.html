Leadership & Setup

Server development and design is being led by Flyn, one of the server's administrators, under the direction of Intravenous. Intravenous joined the Origins community in February 2024 and quickly became one of the most active and successful players, known for running a large market, achieving the highest balance, and becoming the all-time top voter.

Preserving the Past

While the new season starts fresh, the original Origins Reborn worlds will remain preserved. Players will be able to explore them in spectator mode to revisit memories and landmarks. An archived online map is also planned to showcase these original worlds, complete with Towny regions intact for historical reference.

Staff and Moderation

The moderation structure for Origins Rebirth will be separate from the Kingdoms of Aeonis (KoA) server. This ensures both communities have dedicated support without overloading staff. However, builders, artists, developers, and administrators will continue to serve both communities.

Rules remain consistent across both servers: both are family-friendly and maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy toward rule-breaking or boundary-pushing behavior.
