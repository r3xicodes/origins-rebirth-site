Alchemist

Child of a village Librarian and witch, specializing in enchanting, potions, and explosives. Difficulty: 4.

Mobility: Speed 105% (Sneak 30%), Jump 100%, Luck 0, Size 100%
Damage Taken: Melee 120%, Projectiles 120%, Explosion 80%, Magic 80%
Health: 18.0
Arcane Analysis (30s)
Gain experience and change your current enchanting table rolls.
Activation: Use primary ability caster or key.
Catalyst
Modifies potion effects depending on catalyst type.
Activation: Use secondary ability caster or key.
Arcane Shield
Generates shield absorbing damage at experience cost.
Passive perks include potion throwing range and power boosts.

Arachnid

8 legs and eyes, a crawler specialized in climbing and combat. Difficulty: 2.

Mobility: Speed 95% (Sneak 70%), Jump 125%, Luck 0, Size 100%
Damage Taken: Melee 85%, Projectiles 75%, Fire 133%, Explosion 85%
Health: 18.0
Toggle Climb
Allows climbing on walls.
Activation: Use secondary ability key.
Predator
Eats only meat.
Nocturnal
Gains buffs based on moon cycle.
Eighted Armed Architect
Increased combat and build range.

Bee

Buzziest guy around, loves honey and friends with Phytokins. Difficulty: 2.

Mobility: Speed 100% (Sneak 70%), Jump 80%, Luck 0, Size 50%
Damage Taken: Melee 100%, Projectiles 110%, Magic 110%, Fall 50%
Health: 14.0
Pollinate (60s)
Bonemeals saplings, harvests honey and flowers.
Grants temporary bonus health to Phytokins.
Final Sting (Toggle)
1% chance to deal 10x damage at cost of half heart.
Buzz Away
Levitate when crouching; faster movement and lower gravity.
